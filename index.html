About Us

Columbia Machine Learning Group seeks to democratize academic and industrial opportunities to those interested in machine learning and its applications. We are driven by curiosity and ambition to develop machine learning models to better understand foundational underlying data distributions, tackle real-world problems, and explore new frontiers.

As a new organization, we aim to focus on two main areas: Innovation and Education. Given the countless interdisciplinary opportunities in machine learning, we will focus on projects centered around membersâ€™ interests and social impact. In the near future, we plan on collaborating with faculty and industry around New York City. Columbia Machine Learning Group also seeks to provide educational development for the broader Columbia community through hosting career-oriented events with industry and faculty, webinars on machine learning fundamentals, and host interest reading groups.
